## Introduction

We are excited to introduce the new Google Drive Backup feature in our customer portal. This feature allows users to schedule backups directly to their Google Drive, providing an easy and secure way to manage and store important data. Users can not only set up these backups but also access various details related to them, such as scheduling information, Google Drive usage statistics, and comprehensive backup logs.
